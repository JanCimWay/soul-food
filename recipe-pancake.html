Zucchini-Carrot pancakes

Ingidients

  • Zucchini - 2pieces
  • Carrots - 2 pieces
  • Onion - 1/2 pieces
  • Eggs - 2 pieces
  • Gluten free flavour - 1 cup
  • Backing powder - 1/2 tea spoon
  • Garlic powder - 1/4 tea spoon
  • Salt - 1/2 tea spoon
  • Pepper - 1/4 tea spoon

Preperation time

45 minutes

Instructions

Combine shredded zucchinis and carrots , cut the 1/2 of onion and put everything in a bowl. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, baking soda, eggs and mix it all together. Finally, add the flour and combine until a dough like consistency. Heat a large pan with a bit of olive oil or coconut oil (gives the pancakes more taste), use a tablespoon to spoon a better portions, and drop spoonwise into the bottom of the pan. Each side should be golden on the outside . Serve the pancakes with a yoghurt dip. Enjoy!
