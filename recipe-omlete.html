Omelette with ham and cheese

Ingidients

  • 1/2 cup ham in pieces
  • 5 g butter
  • 3 big eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup of cheese (gouda or any other)

Preperation time

20 minutes

Instructions

First of all precook ham and butter on a pan. Meanwhile whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper, and add cream cheese or milk (optional). Whisk until foamy, moderate the heat, choose the medium heat and pour the eggs into the pan over ham. Move eggs around the pan, so that uncooked part of the omlette is also cooked well. Afterwards sprinkle cheese on the top and let it stay for few minutes, so that cheese is melted. Fold the omelette or you also can serve it open with the cheese on top. Serve it immediately and enjoy.
