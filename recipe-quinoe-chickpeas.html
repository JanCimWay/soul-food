Quinoa salad with cheekpeas

Ingidients

  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1/2 of diced red onion
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1/3 cup Olive oil
  • Lemon Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper

Preperation time

35 minutes

Instructions

Cook quinoa (1 cup uncooked quinoa) in a saucepan, bring it to boil, cook about 15-20 minutes, reducing the heat. Remove from the heat, and let it rest for few more minutes. In a large bowl, combine diced cucumbers, bell pepper, red onion and then cut parsley and add the chickpeas. Mix the prepared quinoa and the veggies in a bowl, add few table spoon of an olive oil, salt, pepper, squeeze one half of a lemon and 1/2 tsp of a vinegar. Combine everything and serve.
