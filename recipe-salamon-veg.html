Salmon with roasted vegetables

Ingidients

  • 150-200g fresh salmon fillet
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and papper
  • 2 medium red/orange/yellow bell peppers, cut into cubes
  • 1 small red onion
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 1 small zucchini, cut into cubes/li>
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 2 garlic gloves, minced / for dip
  • 1 cup greek yoghurt / for dip
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt / for dip
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper / for dip

Preperation time

40 minutes

Instructions

Marinate two slices of salmon in a soy sauce, honey and mustard for one hour, preheat the oven, and prepare the veggies. Those can be for example peppers, zucchini, broccoli, cherry tomatoes and onions. Put everything on a sheet pan and the salmon as well, season the veggies and so its ready to go into the oven for 15-20 minutes.Â  Serve it with a yoghurt-garlic dip.
