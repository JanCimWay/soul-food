About

Inspiration

Restaurant „Soul food“ is an art gallery of good. But not in the esthetic or the the beauty, how it is served, but the art is in the combination of the ingredients and in every smallest details, together which creates the artwork of the meal. At the first sight it does not sound, as nothing exclusive or unique, but the concept is that this art of meal is served in a very cosy and home-alike atmosphere, where You don´t have to spend Your energy or Your thoughts about, how do You look, but everything everything You should think about or enjoy, is the combination of tastes.

Our chef

Before starting the restaurant our chef had only love for cooking, but no professional background, and „Soul food“ changed our chefs life. Chef, is the regular way, how You call the most important person in the kitchen. At our kitchen, it is main artist. He's profession, and passion is to find tastes, and new combinations. When traveling, to get something new from the cultures met, local products that are popular and to get something out from the national cosine. All creates, modifies and develops the menu and kitchen of the „Soul Food“
