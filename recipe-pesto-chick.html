Creamy pesto chicken

Ingidients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 40 g pesto
  • 200g Shredded cheese (gouda or mozzarella)

Preperation time

55 minutes

Instructions

You will need to cut the chicken breasts into thiner slices, season the chicken with a pinch of salt and pepper and cook on each side until golden brown. Put the chicken breasts on a sheet pan, coat then with some Philadelphia and pesto on top, sprinkle with shredded cheese and put into the oven for about 30-40 minutes. You can serve it optionally, with brown rice or pasta for example.
