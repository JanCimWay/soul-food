Banana pancakes with berries on top

Ingidients

  • 2 middle ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 vanilla extract
  • 1/4 salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or without
  • 2 tablespoon of buttermilk
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 cup of flour
  • Berries for serving

Preperation time

30 minutes

Instructions

In a bowl mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. In another bowl, combine mashed banana, vanilla extract, an egg, a bit of milk, buttermilk as well for a fluffier consistency. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet ones and mix them until everything is well combined. Let it sit for few minutes and then you can start doing the pancakes. A little bit of butter onto a pan, and then just scoop approximately 1/4 cup of the mixture for each pancake. Cook until you see some airbubbles in a dough and then flip and cook the other side until golden brown. For the berries, heat different kinds of berries in a pan, add a bit of sugar and make a sauce. Serve on a top of the banana pancakes. Ready.
