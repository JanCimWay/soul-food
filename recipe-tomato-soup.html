Roasted tomato soup

Ingidients

  • 9-10 large tomatoes
  • 3-4 garlic gloves
  • 1 big paprika
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon of a heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preperation time

55 minutes

Instructions

All the fresh ingredients : roast onions, any fresh tomatoes, paprika and garlic sprinkle with an oilive oil and herbs and put into the oven for approximately 30-40 minutes. Blend the veggies and the seasonings, such as paprika powder, salt, pepper and sugar and add two cups of broth or stock, to add an extra flavor. Also would recommend adding a bit of a heavy cream, to make the soup creamy. Blend altogether and ready is the soup.Â
