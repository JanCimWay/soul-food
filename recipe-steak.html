Peppered steak with honey glazed carrots

Ingidients

  • Beef steak 500g
  • 1tsp butte
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 salt
  • 5-6 potatoes
  • 20g butter
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • Herbs, salt
  • 350 g mini carrots , trimmed
  • Salt
  • 25g butter
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 1/2tsp lemon juice
  • Pepper

Preperation time

30 minutes

Instructions

Bring water to boil in a little saucepan, add salt. Add carrots and cook 5-7 min until they are tender. Heat a pan with butter, put carrots inside and than add honey and lemon juice. Cook in a lower heat until you see the glaze coated carrots. Meanwhile, cook potatoes in a middle saucepan, until tender, drain and mash and add butter and milk for a creamy consistency. pepper the Steak , heat a pan with butter and garlic cloves and season steak generously. Add into a pan and sear each side till brown (3-4 min.). Add the herbs and sear for one more minute. serve all the components and enjoy
